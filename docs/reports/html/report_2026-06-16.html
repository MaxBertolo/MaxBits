Vibe coding can build your pipeline. It can't explain it six months later

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Spec-driven development (SDD) is a methodology that turns prompts, business rules, and operational context into executable, versioned specifications, integrated directly into the system for persistent memory.
  • Who: Enterprise data engineering teams, increasingly reliant on AI coding agents for rapid pipeline generation, are struggling with fragmented systems and scattered operational context.
  • What it does: SDD converts AI prompts and critical operational knowledge into persistent, versioned system contracts, providing a shared memory for both human engineers and AI agents to ensure consistent evolution.
  • Why it matters: This matters because 'vibe coding' alone fragments critical architectural and business knowledge, hindering system evolution, impact analysis, and efficient coordination across complex, AI-generated data platforms.
  • Strategic view: Implementing SDD offers a strategic imperative to transition from ad-hoc AI-assisted development to a structured, governable, and scalable approach, ensuring long-term system integrity and coordination across fragmented enterprise environments.
