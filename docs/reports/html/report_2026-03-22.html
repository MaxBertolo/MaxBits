Three ways AI is learning to understand the physical world

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: AI researchers are developing "world models" to give AI a foundational understanding of the physical world, moving beyond the abstract knowledge processing capabilities of large language models.
  • Who: AI researchers, including Google DeepMind, NVIDIA, AMI Labs, World Labs, and industry pioneers like Yann LeCun and Demis Hassabis, are driving this development.
  • What it does: These "world models" employ architectures like JEPA for real-time efficiency, Gaussian splats for 3D spatial environments, and end-to-end generation for scalable interactive simulations and synthetic data.
