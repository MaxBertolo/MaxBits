The app store for robots has arrived: Hugging Face launches open-source Reachy Mini App Store with 200+ apps

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Hugging Face launched an open-source "App Store" for its low-cost Reachy Mini robot, offering over 200 community-built applications and tools for non-technical users to create robot behaviors.
  • Who: Hugging Face, known for open-source AI models and tools, developed this platform following its acquisition of Pollen Robotics and the release of its $299 Reachy Mini robot.

  • Strategic view: This move positions Hugging Face as a pivotal player in consumer robotics, aiming to make robots as ubiquitous as smartphones through open-source principles and AI-driven abstraction.
