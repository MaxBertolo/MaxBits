Rapidata emerges to shorten AI model development cycles from months to days with near real-time RLHF

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Rapidata is a platform revolutionizing AI model training by 'gamifying' reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF) through a vast global network of mobile app users.
  • Who: Rapidata is a new AI startup, backed by an $8.5 million seed round, founded by Jason Corkill to transform human judgment collection for AI development.
  • What it does: Rapidata integrates human judgment directly into the AI training loop, allowing models to receive real-time reinforcement learning from global users, drastically accelerating development cycles.
  • Why it matters: It eliminates the bottlenecks of traditional, batch-based human feedback, enabling continuous AI model evolution and addressing subjective quality nuances in generative AI outputs.
  • Strategic view: This paradigm shift transforms human feedback into high-speed infrastructure, accelerating AI innovation, democratizing access to diverse global human intelligence, and enabling more adaptive, ethically grounded AI systems for TMT.

How attackers hit 700 organizations through CX platforms your SOC already approved

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Attackers are exploiting security gaps in Customer Experience (CX) platforms by poisoning data ingested by AI engines, leading to unauthorized access and extensive data theft across numerous organizations.
  • Who: Attackers compromised Salesloft and Drift, impacting over 700 organizations including Cloudflare and Palo Alto Networks, by leveraging legitimate access through approved CX platform integrations.
  • What it does: Attackers inject malicious data into CX platform AI engines, which then trigger automated workflows to exfiltrate sensitive data like AWS keys and passwords from connected payroll, CRM, and payment systems.
  • Why it matters: This highlights critical security blind spots in CX platforms, which are often miscategorized and lack the robust monitoring and posture management applied to other enterprise SaaS, creating significant data loss risks.
  • Strategic view: Organizations must urgently prioritize comprehensive security posture management for CX platforms, integrating continuous monitoring and dedicated policy enforcement to mitigate AI supply chain attacks and prevent widespread data exfiltration.
