The real cost, security, and culture problems behind enterprise AI agents

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: This article details the significant real-world challenges enterprises face when scaling AI agents, specifically focusing on escalating costs, unique security risks, and critical organizational friction.
  • Who: Brian Gracely, Senior Director of Portfolio Strategy at Red Hat, advises enterprises on managing the production realities of AI agents, highlighting the roles of model providers and internal subject matter experts.
