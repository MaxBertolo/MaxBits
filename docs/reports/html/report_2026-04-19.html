Most enterprises can't stop stage-three AI agent threats, VentureBeat survey finds

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: A VentureBeat survey reveals most enterprises lack critical "stage-three" isolation and sandboxing capabilities to secure against advanced AI agent threats like data exposure and supply-chain breaches.
  • Who: VentureBeat surveyed 108 qualified enterprises, with supporting data from Gravitee, Arkose Labs, and experts like CrowdStrike and Enkrypt AI, highlighting industry-wide security gaps.
  • What it does: The survey exposes that most companies are unprepared for AI agent attacks, focusing on monitoring rather than essential isolation and enforcement needed to contain advanced, machine-speed threats.
  • Why it matters: This matters because the pervasive security gap leaves enterprises vulnerable to costly data breaches, regulatory fines, and rapid, uncontainable AI agent attacks that current systems cannot detect or prevent.
  • Strategic view: C-level executives must prioritize architectural shifts towards robust agent isolation, granular identity management for AI, and proactive runtime enforcement to mitigate critical, unaddressed risks from autonomous AI agents.

Anthropic just launched Claude Design, an AI tool that turns prompts into prototypes and challenges Figma

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Anthropic's Claude Design is a new AI tool that transforms conversational prompts into polished visual designs, interactive prototypes, and various marketing collateral.
  • Who: Anthropic launched Claude Design, targeting paid Claude subscribers, directly challenging established design application companies like Figma, Adobe, and Canva.

  • Strategic view: This launch highlights the intensifying AI platform war, as foundation model providers expand vertically, threatening established application layers and forcing TMT companies to re-evaluate their ecosystem strategies.

Salesforce launches Headless 360 to turn its entire platform into infrastructure for AI agents

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Salesforce Headless 360 is an architectural transformation exposing its entire platform via APIs and tools, enabling AI agents to operate the system fully without a graphical user interface.
