Google’s new AI agent can draft your emails, monitor your inbox and eventually spend your money

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Gemini Spark is Google's new personal AI agent, designed to autonomously complete multi-step tasks and work persistently in the cloud, even when devices are locked.
  • Who: Google unveiled Gemini Spark at I/O 2026, competing intensely with Microsoft, OpenAI, Anthropic, and Apple in the race to develop proactive AI agents.
  • What it does: This agent drafts emails, monitors inboxes, assembles documents across applications, and will eventually make autonomous purchases using the new Agent Payments Protocol (AP2).
  • Why it matters: Spark signifies a major shift from reactive AI assistants to proactive, always-on agents that autonomously execute complex tasks, aiming to deliver continuous user value.
  • Strategic view: Google's Gemini Spark establishes a new frontier for autonomous agentic AI, shifting user interaction towards proactive task completion and redefining digital commerce with integrated payment protocols.
