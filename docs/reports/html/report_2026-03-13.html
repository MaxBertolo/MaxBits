Agents need vector search more than RAG ever did

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

The team behind continuous batching says your idle GPUs should be running inference, not sitting dark

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: FriendliAI's InferenceSense platform allows neocloud operators to monetize idle GPU capacity by running paid AI inference workloads, sharing token-based revenue with the operator.
  • Who: FriendliAI, founded by Byung-Gon Chun, who created the foundational continuous batching technique used in AI inference engines like vLLM, developed InferenceSense.

  • Why it matters: This transforms previously unproductive idle GPU cycles into a direct revenue stream for operators, significantly enhancing infrastructure return on investment and potentially lowering overall AI inference costs.
  • Strategic view: TMT executives should view this as a strategic opportunity to optimize GPU capex utilization and generate new revenue from existing infrastructure, potentially impacting AI service pricing and industry competition.
