Fierce Telecom · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Hybrid (Converged) Gateways are advanced broadband devices that integrate multiple connectivity technologies to ensure uninterrupted internet access.
  • Who: Homes and small businesses, increasingly reliant on stable internet for critical daily activities, are the direct beneficiaries of enhanced broadband reliability.
  • What it does: These gateways mitigate service disruptions by seamlessly switching between diverse network infrastructures, thereby maintaining consistent high-speed internet connectivity during outages.
