<a href="https://www.fierce-network.com/sponsored/how-hybrid-converged-gateways-are-redefining-broadband-reliability" hreflang="en">How Hybrid (Converged) Gateways Are Redefining Broadband Reliability</a>

Fierce Telecom · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Hybrid (converged) gateways are innovative network devices designed to maintain internet connectivity by utilizing backup alternatives when primary fiber connections fail.
  • Who: These solutions primarily benefit homes and small businesses that critically depend on uninterrupted, high-speed internet access for their daily operations and leisure.
  • What it does: They automatically switch to alternative network technologies, like cellular, during primary fiber outages caused by events such as storms, ensuring continuous internet service.
  • Why it matters: This technology significantly enhances broadband reliability, mitigating service disruptions that could otherwise severely impact users' work, education, and entertainment activities.
  • Strategic view: For TMT executives, deploying these gateways reduces customer churn, differentiates service offerings, and strengthens brand loyalty by guaranteeing essential connectivity during unforeseen outages.
