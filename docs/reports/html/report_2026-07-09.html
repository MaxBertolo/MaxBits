OpenAI launches GPT-Live, a full-duplex voice upgrade that lets ChatGPT talk more like a person

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: OpenAI has launched GPT-Live, a full-duplex voice upgrade for ChatGPT, fundamentally redesigning how users interact by enabling simultaneous listening and speaking.
  • Who: OpenAI launched GPT-Live globally for all ChatGPT users, with paid tiers getting GPT-Live-1 and free users receiving GPT-Live-1 mini, soon expanding to its API.
  • What it does: GPT-Live introduces a full-duplex architecture for simultaneous conversation and decouples the voice layer from the reasoning layer, enabling continuous interaction while delegating complex tasks asynchronously.
  • Why it matters: This upgrade matters by transforming AI interaction from stilted turn-taking to natural, human-like full-duplex conversation, greatly reducing latency and enhancing user experience across various applications.
  • Strategic view: This modular, full-duplex voice architecture sets a new standard for conversational AI, enabling complex enterprise integrations and paving the way for ubiquitous, natural-sounding voice assistants across Telco, Media, and Tech services.
