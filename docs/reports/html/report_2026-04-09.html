LLM-referred traffic converts at 30-40% — and most enterprises aren't optimizing for it

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) is a new paradigm shifting digital discovery from human-centric search engine rankings to AI agents consuming and citing content directly for synthesized answers.
  • Who: Enterprises, AI agents, and evolving search platforms are fundamentally changing how information is discovered and utilized, moving beyond traditional human-driven web browsing.
  • What it does: AEO shifts content optimization from keyword-based rankings to ensuring AI agents understand, select, and cite enterprise information for direct, synthesized user answers, often bypassing websites.
