Perplexity launches 'Computer' AI agent that coordinates 19 models, priced at $200 a month

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Perplexity's "Computer" is an AI agent orchestration platform that coordinates 19 diverse AI models to autonomously complete complex workflows in the background.
  • Who: Perplexity, a $20 billion valued AI search company, is launching this product for its Perplexity Max subscribers at $200 per month.
