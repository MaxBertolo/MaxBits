Factify wants to move past PDFs and .docx by giving digital documents their own brain

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Factify is a Tel Aviv-based startup developing a new, intelligent digital document format to move beyond static PDFs and collaborative cloud files.
  • Who: Factify, a Tel Aviv-based startup led by Matan Gavish, has secured $73 million to transform digital documents into intelligent infrastructure.
  • What it does: Factify transforms static digital documents into intelligent assets by embedding a unique identity, live permission system, and immutable audit log within them.
  • Why it matters: This matters because it turns documents from static liabilities into verifiable, controllable assets, solving fragmentation and providing structured data essential for AI models.
  • Strategic view: TMT executives should view this as a potential shift in enterprise data infrastructure and content interaction, enabling robust data governance and AI integration.

Airtable's Superagent maintains full execution visibility to solve multi-agent context problem

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Airtable's Superagent is a novel multi-agent AI system that deploys specialized sub-agents to complete complex, unstructured research tasks, building on a data-first philosophy.
  • Who: Airtable, co-founded by Howie Liu, is an established company known for its adaptable, cloud-based relational database platform used by over 500,000 organizations.
  • What it does: Superagent orchestrates parallel teams of specialized AI agents, maintaining full execution visibility and context across tasks to efficiently complete complex, unstructured research.
  • Why it matters: It addresses the multi-agent context problem by ensuring coherent execution, adaptive learning, and reliable results, which significantly improves AI agent performance.
  • Strategic view: Enterprise AI agent success critically depends on strong data architecture, meticulous context orchestration, and leveraging relational database principles for reliable and scalable deployments.

Adaptive6 emerges from stealth to reduce enterprise cloud waste (and it's already optimizing Ticketmaster)

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Adaptive6 is a new cloud cost governance and optimization platform that emerged from stealth to automatically detect and remediate enterprise cloud waste in real-time.
  • Who: Adaptive6 is a new firm co-founded by CEO Aviv Revach, which has raised $44 million in funding, including a Series A led by U.S. Venture Partners (USVP).
  • What it does: Its platform applies a cybersecurity playbook to identify and fix 'Shadow Waste' by tracing cloud resource inefficiencies back to specific code lines and empowering engineers with automated remediation.

  • Strategic view: This platform strategically embeds cloud cost optimization into engineering workflows, enabling TMT firms to proactively manage escalating cloud spend and AI-generated code inefficiencies, much like cybersecurity.
