Nvidia introduces Vera Rubin, a seven-chip AI platform with OpenAI, Anthropic and Meta on board

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • Who: Developed by Nvidia, the platform is adopted by major AI labs like OpenAI, Anthropic, Meta, and cloud providers including AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure.

Nvidia's DGX Station is a desktop supercomputer that runs trillion-parameter AI models without the cloud

VentureBeat – AI · Topic: AI/Cloud/Quantum

  • What it is: Nvidia's DGX Station is a powerful desktop supercomputer enabling local development and operation of large AI models with up to one trillion parameters.
  • Who: Nvidia, a dominant AI chipmaker, unveiled this deskside supercomputer at its GTC conference, targeting developers and enterprises building advanced AI.
  • What it does: This machine provides 20 petaflops of compute and 748GB of unified memory, supporting always-on AI agents and seamless architectural scaling to cloud data centers.
  • Why it matters: It facilitates secure, on-premise AI development and inference, reducing cloud dependency for sensitive data and intellectual property, while lowering long-term operational costs.
  • Strategic view: This offering decentralizes advanced AI capabilities, fostering a hybrid AI strategy where local development and scalable cloud deployment are seamlessly integrated for enhanced security and efficiency.
